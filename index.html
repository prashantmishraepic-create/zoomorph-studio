Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about our services, process, and approach to interior design.

What services does Zoomorph Studio offer?

+

We offer comprehensive interior design and architectural services including residential design, commercial spaces, hospitality design, architectural planning, space optimization, and complete turnkey project management. Our expertise spans from initial concept development to final installation and execution.

How long does a typical interior design project take?

+

Project timelines vary based on scope and complexity. A residential apartment typically takes 2-4 months, while larger commercial or hospitality projects may require 6-12 months. We provide detailed timelines during the consultation phase and maintain transparent communication throughout the project.

What is your design process?

+

Our process begins with an in-depth consultation to understand your vision, requirements, and budget. We then create concept designs and 3D visualizations for your approval. Once finalized, we manage procurement, coordinate with contractors, and oversee installation to ensure flawless execution of the design.

Do you work on projects outside Noida?

+

While we're based in Noida, we regularly undertake projects throughout Delhi NCR including Delhi, Ghaziabad, Greater Noida, and Gurgaon. We also accept select projects in other major cities across India. Contact us to discuss your location-specific requirements.

What is the cost of interior design services?

+

Our pricing is customized based on project scope, size, materials, and complexity. We offer flexible packages to suit various budgets while maintaining our quality standards. Schedule a consultation for a detailed quote tailored to your specific project requirements.

Do you provide 3D visualizations before starting work?

+

Absolutely! We create detailed 3D renderings and visualizations of your space before any work begins. This allows you to see exactly how the finished project will look and make any desired changes to the design before implementation starts.
